# Rubber Bridge Scoring

Complete reference for rubber bridge scoring, verified against ACBL Laws of Rubber Bridge, Wikipedia, BridgeHands, and Pagat.

## Below the Line -- Contract Points (Trick Score)

Only tricks **bid and made** (odd tricks, i.e., tricks over book of 6) count below the line. Overtricks never count below the line.

| Denomination | Undoubled | Doubled | Redoubled |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clubs (per trick) | 20 | 40 | 80 |
| Diamonds (per trick) | 20 | 40 | 80 |
| Hearts (per trick) | 30 | 60 | 120 |
| Spades (per trick) | 30 | 60 | 120 |
| Notrump -- 1st trick | 40 | 80 | 160 |
| Notrump -- each subsequent trick | 30 | 60 | 120 |

Doubling multiplies contract points by 2; redoubling by 4.

**Common game contracts (100+ below the line):**
- 3NT = 40 + 30 + 30 = 100
- 4H/4S = 30 x 4 = 120
- 5C/5D = 20 x 5 = 100
- 2H/2S doubled = 60 x 2 = 120
- 1NT redoubled = 160

## Above the Line -- Overtricks

| Condition | Per Overtrick |
|---|---|
| Undoubled, minor suit | 20 |
| Undoubled, major suit | 30 |
| Undoubled, notrump | 30 |
| Doubled, not vulnerable | 100 |
| Doubled, vulnerable | 200 |
| Redoubled, not vulnerable | 200 |
| Redoubled, vulnerable | 400 |

Undoubled overtricks score at the denomination's normal trick value. Doubled/redoubled overtricks are flat per-trick values based only on vulnerability.

## Above the Line -- Undertrick Penalties

Scored by the defending side.

### Undoubled

| Undertrick | Not Vulnerable | Vulnerable |
|---|---|---|
| Each (flat rate) | 50 | 100 |

### Doubled

| Undertrick | Not Vulnerable | Vulnerable |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 100 | 200 |
| 2nd | 200 | 300 |
| 3rd | 200 | 300 |
| 4th and each subsequent | 300 | 300 |

### Redoubled

Exactly double the doubled values:

| Undertrick | Not Vulnerable | Vulnerable |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 200 | 400 |
| 2nd | 400 | 600 |
| 3rd | 400 | 600 |
| 4th and each subsequent | 600 | 600 |

### Cumulative Doubled Totals (quick reference)

| Down | NV Dbl | V Dbl | NV Rdbl | V Rdbl |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 100 | 200 | 200 | 400 |
| 2 | 300 | 500 | 600 | 1000 |
| 3 | 500 | 800 | 1000 | 1600 |
| 4 | 800 | 1100 | 1600 | 2200 |

| 6 | 1400 | 1700 | 2800 | 3400 |
| 7 | 1700 | 2000 | 3400 | 4000 |

## Above the Line -- Slam Bonuses

Must **bid** the slam level to earn the bonus. Making 12 tricks on a 4-level contract earns overtricks only, not the slam bonus.

| Slam | Not Vulnerable | Vulnerable |
|---|---|---|
| Small slam (6-level) | 500 | 750 |
| Grand slam (7-level) | 1000 | 1500 |

## Above the Line -- Insult Bonus (Making Doubled/Redoubled)

Flat bonus for fulfilling a doubled or redoubled contract. Not affected by vulnerability.

| Condition | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Doubled contract made | 50 |
| Redoubled contract made | 100 |

## Above the Line -- Honors

The five trump honors are A, K, Q, J, 10 of the trump suit. In notrump, honors are the four aces. Must be held in a **single hand** (not split between partners). Either side (declarer or defender) may claim.

| Holding | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Any 4 of 5 trump honors in one hand | 100 |
| All 5 trump honors in one hand | 150 |
| All 4 aces in one hand (notrump only) | 150 |

Many social games omit honors by mutual agreement (no skill involved). Honors do not exist in duplicate bridge.

## Game and Rubber Structure

### Game
- A side wins a game when they accumulate **100+ contract points below the line**.
- Part-scores carry forward across deals within a game.
- When either side wins a game, a **horizontal line is drawn** under both sides. Both sides start the next game at 0 below the line. (Previous part-score points still count in the final total, just not toward the next game.)

### Vulnerability
- A side that has won **one game** is **vulnerable**.
- A side that has won **zero games** is **not vulnerable**.
- Vulnerability affects: doubled/redoubled overtricks, all undertrick penalties, and slam bonuses.

### Rubber
- A rubber is won by the first side to win **two games** (best of three).
- The rubber ends immediately when one side wins their second game.

### Rubber Bonus

| Outcome | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Won rubber 2-0 | 700 |
| Won rubber 2-1 | 500 |

### Unfinished Rubber/Game Bonuses

If play stops before a rubber is completed:

| Situation | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Side has won the only completed game | 300 |
| Side has the only part-score in an unfinished game | 100 |

## Final Scoring

At the end of the rubber, each side totals **all** points (above and below the line combined). The side with the higher total wins. The margin of victory is the difference.

## Summary: What Goes Where

**Below the line (toward game):**
- Contract points for tricks bid and made (only)

**Above the line (bonuses and penalties):**
- Overtricks
- Undertrick penalties (scored by defending side)
- Slam bonuses
- Insult bonus (50 doubled, 100 redoubled)
- Honors
- Rubber bonus (700 or 500)
- Unfinished rubber/game bonuses (300 or 100)
